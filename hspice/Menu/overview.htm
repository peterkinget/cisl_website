Directions on how to use HSPICE

(URL: http://www.cisl.columbia.edu/HSPICE/index.html)

This website provides a brief overview of how undergraduate students in electrical engineering at Columbia University may access and use our HSPICE circuit simulation software. 

To become familiar with the basic features and capabilities of the software, you are strongly advised to first read through the material provided under the above link [Basics]. 

To run HSPICE you will, of course, need to have access to the software.  Follow the above link [Access] to find a description of how you may obtain that.

To get a feeling of what HSPICE can do for you, and the steps involved in using it, you are advised to complete the [Tutorial].

To investigate in depth a particular subject, you may want to research the complete HSPICE manual.  Each individual chapter is available online in pdf format through the above link [Manual].

 

The material in this website is an expanded version of material presented by Dr. J. Steensgaard. This website is maintained by Shouri Chatterjee.  This page was last updated on 02/11/2003
