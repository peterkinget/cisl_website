Courses

The courses below are often thaught by CISL faculty and relate to electronics and integrated circuit design. The 3000-level courses are typically taken by B.S. juniors, the 4000-level courses are open to B.S. seniors, M.S. and Ph.D. students and the 6000-level courses are graduate courses.

To see our course offerings for the current semester visit the EE department Courses Webpage or the EE department Online Bulletin. Dependencies of the courses are shown on the Course Chart.
