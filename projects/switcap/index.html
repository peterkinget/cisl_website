New Features in SWITCAP3 (Currently in development by Ilya Yusim)
  • Noise Analysis of Ideal Switched Capacitor Networks
  • Switched Current Simulation (Frequency and Time Domain)

    Switcap3, in addition to Switcap2 features, will have noise analyses for ideal switched-capacitor circuits. It will also provide frequency and time domain simulations for switched circuits that contain MOSFETS but still completely settle within each switching interval. This includes switched-current circuits and switched capacitor circuits containing non-ideal op-amps or capacitors. (Noise analyses for such circuits will not be available.) We plan to provide the SPICE Level 3 and BSIM3v3 MOSFET models.
