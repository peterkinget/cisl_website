Undergraduate & M.S. Student Research Involvement

  • Our group has an active program of undergraduate and M.S. student research involvement during the semester and summer and we also participate in the School's Undergraduate Reseach Involvement Program (URIP).
  • If you are an Columbia Undergraduate, Master's, or Ph.D. student interested in doing a project (for credit) that involves designing, simulating, building and/or measuring Analog or RF hardware or Integrated Circuits, contact Prof. Kinget.

EE Student Projects Lab

  • The EE deparment is happy to announce the Student Projects Lab. This lab is open to EE and CE students of all levels to pursue their own projects. See the announcement of the chair for details. The opening of the lab was also featured in the Columbia Campus Newspaper 'The Spectator'. Follow this link or take a look at a the article in pdf.

IEEE-MTT Fellowships

  • The IEEE Microwave Theory and Techniques Society offers undergraduate fellowships for senior undergraduate students who want to do a research project. Details can be found from the society's homepage www.mtt.org -> Awards -> Undergraduate Scholarships. Contact Prof. Kinget if you are interested to apply.
