Advanced Projects Opportunity

Interested in working on a challenging project with state-of-the-art design tools, characterization equipment and devices fabricated in advanced integrated circuit process technology?

Wireless Testing of Integrated Circuits

We have developed a methodology for the wireless test of integrated circuits. Such methodology will result in very significant cost reductions in the test and debugging of advanced integrated circuits.

We are looking for highly motivated students interested in doing a research project on:

  • device testing,
  • integrated circuit design,
  • wireless and RF design.

This project gives you a unique opportunity of learning about advanced issues in device testing and debugging, RF and wireless design and integrated circuit design. The goal is to work out a testing method and its circuit implemenation, to do circuit simulations and layout, fabrication and experimental characterization (the whole cycle requires 2 semesters).

Intrigued? Interested?

Contact: Prof. P. Kinget

Open for qualified B.S, M.S. or Ph.D students; 1 or group of 2 students

This project can be done for credit.
