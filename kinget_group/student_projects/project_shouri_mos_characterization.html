Interested in working on a project with state-of-the-art equipment and devices fabricated in modern integrated circuit process technology?


CMOS Characterization for ultra-low voltage circuit design


This project will give you a taste of solid state device characterization, fundamental concepts of circuit testing and measurements. It will give you an opportunity to work with state-of-the-art equipment. The devices have been fabricated in a 0.18 micron CMOS process and are suited to ultra-low-voltage operation.


The project involves characterizing the CMOS devices, comparing the results with simulations and time permitting, developing models based on these characteristics.
