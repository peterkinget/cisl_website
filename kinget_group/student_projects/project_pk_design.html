Interested in learning about the design of analog or RF integrated circuits?


Analog/RF integrated circuit design


If you are interested in the design of analog or RF integrated circuits, this project offers you the opportunity to learn about the full design cycle. The project starts with the design and simulation of the circuit. Depending on the available time it can include the layout of the (integrated) circuit and the submission for fabrication. IC fabrication typically takes 12 weeks. The experimental verification of the chip can be done in a follow-up project.


Contact: Prof. P. Kinget, kinget AT ee.columbia.edu, #4-0309, 818 CEPSR


Open for qualified senior B.S, M.S. or Ph.D students; familiarity with

analog circuit design and with design tools is necessary; 1 or group of 2 students


This project can be done for credit.
