Description:

This course introduces some of the basic microwave design principles and discusses their application in high-speed microwave and digital circuits. The course introduces transmission line theory and impedance matching techniques, microwave circuit network analysis and coupled line theory. It gives an overview of active and passive microwave components and discusses the design of practical microwave circuits such as low-noise, broadband and power amplifiers.
